The Trend List

Tracking what’s trending on Not On The High Street using real customer review data.

What is The Trend List?

The Trend List is a data-led view of what’s actually trending on Not On The High Street.

Instead of curated edits or paid placements, we analyse real customer behaviour — using review activity to identify which products and brands are gaining traction right now.

How it works

Each month we analyse thousands of products and brands across the marketplace, tracking:

  • Review activity and momentum
  • Changes in ranking over time
  • Brand performance and growth

This allows us to highlight:

  • Trending products right now
  • Top-performing brands
  • Emerging products gaining attention

Why this exists

Visibility is one of the biggest challenges for independent brands.

The Trend List exists to make discovery easier — helping customers find genuinely popular products, while giving smaller brands a fairer chance to be seen.
