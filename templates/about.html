About Most Reviews

Most Reviews by Wue is a platform designed to celebrate and support small businesses — especially in a world increasingly shaped by giants like Amazon, Temu, and Shein.

Using our own AI-powered tools, we’ve built a growing database of independent sellers on Not On The High Street, highlighting the most loved products and the most reviewed shops over the past 12 months.

This site is updated monthly to spotlight what’s trending, what’s working, and what customers are raving about.

We’re huge fans of the NOTHS community and created Most Reviews to amplify the amazing work of its sellers.

If you’re a brand or organisation that would like access to more detailed insights — or want to work together — we’d love to hear from you at info@mostreviews.co.uk.
