About the Data

How we collect, analyse, and rank the most reviewed products and partners on Not On The High Street.

Most Reviews analyses publicly available data from the Feefo customer review platform to identify the products and partners receiving the highest number of verified reviews on Not On The High Street.

Our rankings are based on the total number of verified customer reviews rather than average star ratings. Review data is refreshed monthly, with the latest update published on the 1st of each month.

While we make every effort to ensure accuracy, listings and review totals can change at any time. Most Reviews is an independent project and is not affiliated with Not On The High Street or Feefo.
